About
Dr. G. P. Venkat Choudary

Consultant Interventional Radiologist Yashoda Hospitals, Secunderabad

Transforming Healthcare with Interventional Radiology

Dr. G. P. Venkat Choudary, a Consultant Interventional Radiologist at Yashoda Hospitals, Secunderabad, brings a wealth of expertise to his practice. Graduating from Siddhartha Medical College, Vijayawada, and further honing his skills with an MD in Radiology from Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences (SVIMS), Tirupati, he pursued specialized training in Neuro and Vascular Interventional Radiology at Max Super Specialty Hospital, Saket, New Delhi. Dr. Venkat Choudary’s dedication to advancing patient care and his comprehensive skill set make him a trusted name in interventional radiology.


Oncological Interventions: TACE, TARE, Thermal Ablations, Liver Transplant Interventions

Vascular Interventions: Uterine Fibroid Embolization, Prostatic Artery Embolization, GI Bleed Embolization

Neurovascular & Other Procedures: Osteoid Osteoma Ablations, Neurovascular Interventions
